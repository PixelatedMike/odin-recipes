Honey Garlic Chicken Recipe

Description

yo one time i was really hungry and all we had in the fridge was some uh chicken breast and i
looked up good chicken breast recipes and found this. fed me and my sister goated recipe fr fr

Ingredients

4 servings

  • olive oil (for pan)
  • 1.5 pounds chicken breast, cut into small cubes
  • 3 tbsp honey
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • black pepper
  • minced garlic
  • (optional) pinch of red pepper flakes
  • (optional) green onions, diced
  • (optional) sesame seeds

Directions

  1. Oil up the pan and set to medium-high heat
  2. Season the cubed chicken lightly with black pepper
  3. Add the chicken to the pan and brown on one side (3-4 min)
  4. Make the glaze by whisking honey, soy sauce, minced garlic, red pepper flakes
  5. Turn the chicken pieces over. Add the sauce to the pan and toss to coat the chicken pieces.
  6. Serve. Optional: top with green onions, sesame seeds, lime juice?
